\section{Logarithms}
Paper 1 and 8.

The properties of logarithms are:
\begin{equation}
A^P = N \implies P = \log_A N, if A \neq \{0, 1\}
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
A^{\log_{A}{N}} = N
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
\log_A(M * N) = \log_A N + \log_A M
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
\log_A \frac{M}{N} = \log_A N - \log_A M
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
\log_A M^P = p * \log_A M
\end{equation}
\begin{equation}
\log_A N = \log_B N - \log_B A
\end{equation}
